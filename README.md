## 📖 About
**SBS Ad Skipper** automatically fast-forwards through ad breaks on SBS On Demand movies and episodes, then restores normal playback as soon as your show resumes.

Because SBS On Demand uses server-side ad insertion (SSAI), ads cannot simply be blocked like traditional web advertisements. Instead, this extension detects when an ad break is playing, temporarily mutes the player, speeds playback up, and restores everything when the content returns.

## ✨ Features
* **Auto-Fast-Forward:** Skips SBS On Demand ad breaks automatically.
* **Smart Restoration:** Resumes normal playback as soon as your content returns.
* **Mute During Ads:** Automatically mutes audio while advertisements are accelerated.
* **User Control:** Toolbar toggle to enable or disable the extension whenever you choose.
* **Statistics:** Includes a local lifetime ad-break counter.
* **Privacy-First:** Lightweight and does not track your viewing habits.

## ⚠️ Limitations
* Works only on SBS On Demand on-demand titles.
* Does not work on live streams or live sports.
* Does not bypass authentication, subscriptions, DRM, or content restrictions.
* Does not block, intercept, or modify network traffic.

## 🔒 Privacy
**No data is collected, transmitted, sold, or shared.**

Only the following information is stored locally:
* Extension enabled/disabled state
* Local ad-break counter

*All information remains strictly on your device.*
