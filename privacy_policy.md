# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

SBS Ad Skipper does not collect, transmit, store, share, sell, or process any personal information.

## Information Stored

The extension stores only:

* Whether the extension is enabled or disabled.
* A local counter showing how many ad breaks have been skipped.

This information is stored locally using Chrome's extension storage and is used solely for the extension's functionality.

## Information Not Collected

SBS Ad Skipper does not collect:

* Names
* Email addresses
* Passwords
* Browsing history
* Viewing history
* IP addresses
* Device identifiers
* Analytics data
* Usage statistics
* Any personally identifiable information

## Data Sharing

No information is shared with any third party.

## Network Activity

The extension does not intercept, modify, block, inspect, or transmit network traffic.

The extension operates entirely within the SBS On Demand webpage by temporarily adjusting video playback speed during detected advertisement breaks.

## Third-Party Services

SBS Ad Skipper does not use:

* Analytics services
* Tracking services
* Advertising services
* External APIs

## Changes

This policy may be updated in future versions. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the extension's support page.
